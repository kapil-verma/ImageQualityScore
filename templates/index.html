Automated Image Quality Estimation Model


The model can estimate two type of scores: Aesthetic and Technical, based on the input.
Aesthetic score quantifies semantic level characteristics associated with emotions and beauty in images whereas technical score quantifies quality metrics such as noise, blur, compression etc.

Architecture : Mobilenet, Backend : Flask

Instructions:

1. A new page will appear after uploading the image, selecting score type & clicking submit. It may take upto a minute (maximum)

2. You will get a JSON dictionary of selected score result out of 10.
